About Motivio

Motivio is a user-friendly web application designed to help car owners track their vehicle's maintenance history and manage upcoming service needs through manual data entry.

Our Mission

We believe that every car owner should have easy access to their vehicle's maintenance history. Our mission is to provide a simple, effective tool that helps you keep your car running smoothly and avoid costly repairs through proper maintenance tracking.

Who We Are

Motivio was created by a team of car enthusiasts and software developers who wanted a better way to track their vehicle maintenance. The team consists of Grace Costello, Aydin Gursoy, and Noman Afzal.

Key Features

Vehicle Profiles

Add and manage multiple vehicles with detailed information including make, model, year, and VIN.

Maintenance Log

Record maintenance activities, upload receipts, and keep a complete service history.

Service Reminders

Set up custom reminders for upcoming maintenance based on mileage or time intervals.

Mileage Tracker

Log mileage periodically to track usage patterns and predict upcoming maintenance.
